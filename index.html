About Us

Welcome to 098 by Fredes, where culinary excellence meets innovation and authenticity. Our vision is to create a universal dining experience that celebrates the diversity of global cuisine while delivering top-notch service both in our restaurant and through our extensive delivery options.

We believe in the power of food to bring people together and to create memorable experiences. Our menu is a testament to this belief, offering a wide range of categories from Beef, Seafood and Chicken to Vegan, and Vegetarian dishes. We also pride ourselves on our extensive cocktail list, featuring everything from classic Ordinary Drinks and Cocktails to unique Homemade Liqueurs and Coffee. We strive to be more than just a place to eat; we aim to be a cornerstone of culinary artistry and a beacon of innovation in the dining scene. Whether you're enjoying a meal in our inviting restaurant or savoring a dish delivered to your door, we are committed to providing an exceptional dining experience that stays true to our roots and values.

Join us at 098, and discover a world of flavors, where every meal is a celebration of taste, tradition, and togetherness!

Our Story:

098 was founded in 2023 by Vicente Fredes Estuardo, driven by a passion for culinary diversity and innovation. Starting as a small project, Vicente aimed to create a unique dining experience that offered a wide array of flavors. The restaurant quickly gained a reputation for its diverse menu and exceptional service.

With the addition of General Manager Gladys Marin Millie and Culinary Director Olga Montini, 098 expanded its vision. Executive Chef Caroline Polachek and Sous Chef Fiona Apple McAfee brought their culinary expertise, crafting a menu that spans multiple cuisines.

Today, 098 stands as a beacon of culinary excellence, offering both dine-in and delivery services that celebrate the fusion of tradition and modernity.
